Mossy Cloak


Laura Hart Newlon
Untitled (Plumb Line)
2013
40" x 50"

Camouflage is a tool. It hijacks ingrained ways of perception to operate within, under and through them. Mossy Cloak, curated by Third Object, is a group exhibition of work in photography, painting, sound and sculpture from six artists. It examines the layered vocabularies of camouflage, exploring its role as a visual surface patterning and as metaphor for social and historical concealment.

Samantha Bittman
Drew Broderick
Liz Ensz
Kiam Marcelo Junio
Tim McMullen
Laura Hart Newlon
